About

We are Gratler. It is a platform that makes your traveling easier and inexpensive, rather free. Yes! you heard that right. Gratler is a tech company which primarily focuses on connecting the physical and digital world to facilitate movement at an ease of a click. We are targeting enhanced security, safety and we support your right to move and work freely and without fear, regardless of your gender, colour, religion, ability, or sexual orientation.

About Us

GRATLER comes from Latin word "GRATIS" meaning Given Freely and "LER" is from the word Traveller which means Free Travelling.

The kind of people who are tenacious in their pursuit of our purpose to assist people in going anywhere, obtaining anything, and paving their own way. What we power is motion. It is vital to us . It forces us to consistently rethink how we can move more effectively.  For every location you wish to visit. For anything you wish to obtain. For every method of income you desire. everywhere in the world. actual time at the present's astounding speed.

We are in our incipient stage but wish to aim that every person can travel at minimal or no cost. The idea came into existence when the founders were traveling to a fest and they thought that the issues of parking, space management, time, traffic and accidents could all be alleviated if the ones who brought cars or even the bikers would provide a carpool service. This seems common isn't it? So then what if the traveling is free of cost? The idea of carpooling free of cost and helping the ones who provide lifts by providing them with free traveling whenever they need resulted in GRATLER.

  • Security

    At Gratler, the well-being of our customers is above everything else. We are constantly in pursuit of enhancing our safety measures to ensure every Rapido ride is a pleasant and comfortable experience. Every woman rider's contact number is masked to protect their privacy and their interaction with the Captain happens only through app encryption.

    The site has an SOS button which is a 24*7 support feature. This helps the rider connect with an emergency team that is backed by on-ground support in every city. Wherever required, the emergency team is equipped to get in touch with the nearest police station.

  • Customers

    Trip Sharing-Riders can share their trip status with their respective emergency contacts on Gratler right after their ride starts.

  • Captain Information

    Once the ride is booked, the following details of the Captain are accessible to the riders: Captain's name, Vehicle number, Vehicle model name, Past ride record, Star rating given by other rider.

    Post the ride, both parties can give a rating to each other and any rating below 3 is flagged from GRATLER's end. GRATLER reaches out to them in 5-10 minutes to address their concern. Stict actions are taken against the people who are accused of misconduct.
